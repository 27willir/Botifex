Exactly How Botifex Works

Every Botifex plan starts with payment so you can unlock premium refresh speeds immediately. This guide shows the entire consumer journey—from checkout, to account creation, to marketplace domination.

Start Your Pro Plan Review Pricing
1

Select Plan & Pay First

Visit the payment flow, choose Free, Standard, or Pro, and complete secure checkout powered by Stripe.

  • Plan selection happens before account creation.
  • Billing email becomes the anchor for your forthcoming Botifex profile.
  • Pro plan unlocks the entire marketplace suite described on the Overview page.
2

Finish Account Creation

After payment, you’re taken to a streamlined sign-up form.

  • Provide first name, optional last name, phone number, ZIP code, email, and password.
  • Review why we request location and phone via the Why We Collect This Information policy link beside the form.
  • Agree to terms, set your password, and your Botifex account becomes active.
3

Configure Searches

Use the dashboard to add keywords, price ranges, and target locations.

  • Each plan enforces refresh intervals listed on the Pricing page.
  • Saved searches power real-time alerts and the daily digest email.
4

Get Alerts & Take Action

Alerts arrive via email or SMS (Plan dependent). Favorite listings, add notes, or share directly.

  • Contact support anytime from the Contact page.
  • Future enhancements appear on the Roadmap, keeping you in the loop.
