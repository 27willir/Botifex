The Botifex Story

Botifex exists because manually refreshing marketplaces was wasting hours every week. A 16-year-old builder in rural Idaho hacked together the first version for personal use. Today Botifex is a polished platform serving consumers across the U.S.

Start Your Pro Plan Reach out to the team

From Raspberry Pi to Platform

Learning to code quickly, the founder built five prototypes in twelve days before landing on the Botifex approach.

There was no venture funding or giant team—just determination and a desire to find better deals faster.

Consumers First

Every feature is evaluated by how it helps individual buyers. Botifex is not framed as “AI automation for businesses.”

Instead, we obsess over refresh speed, trustworthy alerts, and analytics that empower shoppers.

Our Core Values

These values guide what ships next on the Roadmap.

Speed

Listings must reach consumers before they disappear. Infrastructure investments prioritize refresh intervals.

Clarity

Every alert includes context, pricing, and source links. No fluff, only action-ready information.

Trust

Payment-first flow keeps fraudsters out. Privacy policies, including Why We Collect This Information, remain transparent.

Milestones

  • 2024: Prototype V5 launches on a Raspberry Pi. Real-time Craigslist and eBay alerts prove the model.
  • 2025: Botifex LLC formed, multi-marketplace support added, payment-first flow introduced.
  • Today: Continuous shipping of consumer features; next up is mobile, smarter deals, and global support.
