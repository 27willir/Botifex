How Price Alerts Work

  • Keywords: Enter keywords to monitor (e.g., "Corvette", "iPhone 15")
  • Alert Type: Choose "under" to get alerts when prices drop below your threshold, or "over" for prices above
  • Automatic Monitoring: The system checks every 5 minutes for new listings
  • Notifications: You'll receive email/SMS based on your notification preferences
  • Rate Limiting: Each alert triggers once per hour maximum to prevent spam
